# Moving Quote Offer Shortlist 2026-06-10

## Decision

Use `moving-quote` as the first monetization test theme.

Launch angle:

> 이사 견적 전화폭탄 줄이는 3분 준비표

The offer should not promise that calls will never happen. The safer promise is:

> 견적 신청 전에 정보를 정리하고, 비교 업체 수와 상담 포인트를 통제한다.

## Why This Category Can Monetize

Moving quote is a strong CPA/CPC category because the user is close to an expensive offline purchase and needs a provider match.

The user already has intent:

- moving date,
- origin and destination,
- home size,
- moving type,
- need for packing, storage, cleaning, internet, or appliances.

That makes a quote request valuable to moving platforms and partner vendors.

## Offer Selection Rules

Pick the first offer that satisfies all of these:

1. Public or approved partner terms are available.
2. Blog/SNS/search/pre-lander traffic is allowed.
3. The action is measurable: click, quote request, consultation request, or install.
4. The page can honestly describe the action.
5. Required disclosure text is known.
6. No forbidden claims are needed to convert.

Reject offers that require:

- fake urgency,
- unverified "lowest price" claims,
- hiding that the destination is a partner/affiliate service,
- collecting personal data locally before privacy copy is ready,
- automated posting, comments, DMs, or cafe spam.

## Recommended First Offer Path

1. Check Adpot/DBSense campaign details for `[기타] 서경석의 이사방`.
2. Check Adlix campaign details for `다이사 이사 가격비교`.
3. Check Tenping dashboard for moving or living-service campaigns.
4. If networks are blocked, contact Daisa/Miso/AJD/24Story for a tracked partner link.

Do not build the first MVP around direct form capture. Use outbound CTA tracking first.

Reason:

- faster launch,
- lower privacy burden,
- easier approval with affiliate networks,
- enough to validate click intent before handling personal information.
