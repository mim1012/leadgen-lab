## Why This Can Make Money

The monetization mechanism is not "a landing page makes money by existing."

The mechanism is:

1. A user has a high-intent problem.
2. The user searches or clicks content about that problem.
3. The pre-lander explains the decision, risk, and checklist better than a raw affiliate link.
4. The user clicks a CTA to a CPA, affiliate, or partner offer.
5. The partner pays if the user performs the approved action, such as quote request, consultation request, fair application, or purchase.

In this model, the pre-lander earns money indirectly by increasing qualified outbound actions.

## Why Decision-Support Pages Work Better Than Generic Quiz Pages

Generic quiz page:

- creates curiosity,
- may get clicks,
- often lacks purchase intent,
- can feel unrelated to the final CPA offer.

Decision-support pre-lander:

- starts from an active buying or inquiry moment,
- reduces a specific fear before personal-information submission,
- gives the user language for comparing vendors,
- makes the CTA feel like the next logical step.

Examples:

- Wedding: "Before submitting your phone number, check consent scope and discount conditions."
- Moving: "Before requesting quotes, prepare inventory and choose a limited-contact comparison path."
- Interior: "Before consultation, define scope and questions to avoid added cost surprises."
