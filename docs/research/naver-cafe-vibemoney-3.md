## Observed Funnel Pattern

The article describes a lead-generation funnel:

1. Find a CPA/affiliate campaign that pays for qualified consultation leads.
2. Build a narrow landing page with an AI coding tool.
3. Add viral/interactive content instead of a plain information page.
4. Use Threads, SEO, and optional low-cost ads to drive traffic.
5. Route visitors through the landing page before the final campaign destination.
6. Iterate many small sites and angles quickly.

## Tactical Notes

- Example campaign theme in the article: wedding/event-related lead generation.
- Example viral wrapper: relationship/tarot content with generated visual assets.
- Mentioned infrastructure pattern: Netlify for static deployment and Neon for a free database tier.
- Comment thread includes keyword/ad angle: generate many low-cost keyword variants for search ads.
- Comment thread also highlights a restriction issue: some CPA offers prohibit direct SNS promotion, implying that campaign rules must be recorded and reviewed before launch.

## Product Implications

Leadgen Lab should focus on a compliant workbench, not blind automation:

- campaign restriction registry,
- persona and angle generator,
- viral landing brief generator,
- static landing page builder,
- UTM/tracking planner,
- screenshot/evidence capture,
- human approval before deployment or traffic publication.

## MVP Recommendation

Start with a low-risk demo funnel:

- category: relationship/tarot test
- goal: validate landing-page generation and content planning
- data capture: avoid personal lead forms initially; use outbound CTA tracking first
- traffic plan: manually reviewed Threads content calendar

Avoid finance/debt/medical-adjacent lead capture until compliance templates and disclosure review exist.
