# Community Deep Research: Leadgen Lab

## Status

- Session started: 2026-06-10
- Mode: new research
- Topic: community-driven opportunity research for Leadgen Lab launch themes
- Current phase: initial source scan and hypothesis refinement

## Research Question

Which lead-generation landing experiment should Leadgen Lab launch first if generic "wedding/tarot test" ideas are too common?

## Working Scope

Geography:

- Primary: Korea
- Secondary: global English communities where indie builders discuss monetization and landing pages

Communities and source types:

- Naver Cafe and Blog
- Threads
- Reddit
- Blind and other Korean public community pages when accessible
- YouTube/Instagram only as supporting signals
- Official policy pages for platform and disclosure constraints

## Early Signal

The stronger opportunity may not be "make a tarot landing page." It may be:

> Help users make a safer decision before they submit personal information into high-pressure consultation funnels.

Examples:

- wedding fair comparison before phone-number submission,
- moving quote comparison before multiple vendors call,
- interior estimate checklist before site visits,
- rental appliance comparison before sales calls.

## Initial Hypotheses

### H1: Wedding fair privacy-safe comparison

Users want wedding information, but they distrust promotional content and dislike repeated sales calls after submitting phone numbers.

Potential product angle:

- "웨딩박람회 가기 전 3분 체크리스트"
- "전화폭탄 피하는 웨딩박람회 비교 가이드"
- "내 상황에 맞는 웨딩 상담 유형 진단"

Leadgen Lab angle:

- pre-lander creates trust by helping users decide what to ask before submitting information.

### H2: Moving quote decision assistant

Moving users struggle to compare quotes and fear price inconsistency, coordination burden, and unreliable vendors.

Potential product angle:

- "이사 견적 받기 전 체크리스트"
- "포장이사/반포장이사 선택 테스트"
- "이사 견적 비교 질문지 생성기"

Leadgen Lab angle:

- more utility-driven than viral, possibly stronger search intent.

### H3: Interior estimate readiness checker

Interior customers need to define scope, budget, and style before consultation. Poor preparation causes confusing quotes and bad vendor selection.

Potential product angle:

- "우리집 인테리어 견적 준비도 테스트"
- "업체 상담 전 질문 리스트 생성기"
- "추가비용 방지 체크리스트"

Leadgen Lab angle:

- high-value lead category, but quality and trust requirements are higher.

### H4: Vibe-coding monetization is a meta-market

Builders can make landing pages quickly but struggle with positioning, marketing, and first users.

Potential product angle:

- "바이브코딩 프로젝트 첫 100명 유입 플랜"
- "랜딩페이지 + 콘텐츠 캘린더 + 커뮤니티 배포 체크리스트"

Leadgen Lab angle:

- sell the tool to builders rather than using the tool only for CPA campaigns.

## Source Leads

- Reddit r/vibecoding discussions repeatedly say building is easier than selling and marketing.
- Reddit r/Entrepreneur and r/SideProject discussions emphasize validating demand with simple landing pages before building full products.
- Korean wedding fair sources and personal blogs mention promotional overload, phone calls, and difficulty finding genuine information.
- Official Meta/Threads policies require avoiding spammy or manipulative distribution and observing branded-content/disclosure requirements.
